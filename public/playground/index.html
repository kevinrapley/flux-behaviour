Playground

The full behavioural model, live

This playground carries over the whole of the original Flux Behavioural Analytics prototype: all 20 dimensions, the v46.s scoring engine, pointer kinematics, navigation analysis, typing behaviour, governance actions, personas and composites. Complete the realistic form below however you naturally would — every interaction is measured and every measurement moves scores.

Scores describe interaction patterns for service improvement. They must never be read as judgements about a person's competence, honesty or ability.
