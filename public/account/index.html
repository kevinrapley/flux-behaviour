Sign in to Flux Behaviour

Use the Google account that your Flux workspace administrator has granted access to. Flux does not collect your Google password or use email one-time codes.

To authorise dashboard access, Flux processes and stores your verified Google email address and Google provider account identifier. Account identity is kept separate from pseudonymous behavioural analytics identifiers.
